Our Services

From farm equipment to classic cars, residential to commercial — if it needs stripping, we handle it.

Automotive & Trailer

Classic car restoration, trailer refurbishing, fleet vehicle maintenance. We strip paint, rust, and undercoating without warping panels.

Commercial & Residential

Building facades, concrete surfaces, brick restoration, graffiti removal. Gentle enough for historic structures, powerful enough for industrial.

Industrial & Farm

Heavy equipment, tractors, implements, structural steel, tanks, and piping. We restore surfaces to bare metal for proper recoating.

Marine

Boat hulls, pontoons, dock structures, marine hardware. Remove antifouling paint, oxidation, and corrosion safely near waterways.

Built For The Tough Jobs

Show Me Mobile Blasting delivers professional dustless blasting services across the Midwest. We come to your location with our equipment, saving you the hassle and expense of transporting your project.

Our process combines water and recycled glass media at high pressure to strip paint, rust, corrosion, and coatings without the harmful dust clouds of traditional sandblasting. It's faster, safer, and better for the environment.
