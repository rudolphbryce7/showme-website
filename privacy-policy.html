Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

ShowMe Mobile Blasting LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit showmemobileblasting.com, interact with our contact forms, or engage with our business through any channel.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out a quote request or contact form on our website
  • Interact with our live chat widget
  • Click on or respond to one of our Facebook or Instagram advertisements
  • Call, text, or email us directly

The information collected may include your name, phone number, email address, location, and a description of your project or service needs.

We may also collect standard website usage data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, and IP address through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry
  • Contact you to schedule a free estimate or service appointment
  • Follow up on your request via phone, text, or email
  • Improve the quality and relevance of our services

We may use your phone number to send SMS communications, including marketing, transactional, and informational messages, to users who have explicitly opted in via our website forms. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to (573) 410-4010.

We do not send mass marketing emails, add you to mailing lists, or make unsolicited sales calls to lists of contacts. We only contact people who have directly reached out to us or responded to one of our advertisements.

3. SMS Messaging

When you submit a form on our website and check the SMS consent box, you are opting in to receive text messages from ShowMe Mobile Blasting. These messages may include quote follow-ups, appointment confirmations, and service-related communications.

  • Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and service status
  • Msg & data rates may apply
  • Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe
  • Reply HELP for assistance
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase

4. Facebook and Instagram Advertising

We run advertisements on Facebook and Instagram to reach people in our service area who may be interested in mobile blasting services. If you click on one of our ads and fill out a form, or if you visit our website after seeing an ad, your information is collected and handled as described in this policy.

This is a lead generation process, not a marketing list operation. When you submit your information, you are opting in to be contacted by ShowMe Mobile Blasting specifically — and only in response to your expressed interest. We do not use your information for retargeting campaigns run on behalf of third parties.

Facebook and Instagram have their own privacy policies that govern how they collect and use data on their platforms. We encourage you to review Meta's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Live Chat Widget

Our website uses a live chat widget powered by GoHighLevel. When you initiate a chat, the information you provide (name, phone, email, and message) is captured and stored in our CRM so we can respond to your inquiry. This constitutes an opt-in to be contacted by us regarding your request.

6. Contact Forms and Quote Requests

When you submit a form on our website, you are opting in to be contacted by ShowMe Mobile Blasting via phone, text message, or email in response to your request. Standard message and data rates may apply for text communications. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or by contacting us directly.

7. CRM and Data Storage

Your contact information and inquiry details are stored in GoHighLevel, a CRM platform we use to manage customer relationships and scheduling. GoHighLevel operates under its own privacy and data security standards. Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to ShowMe Mobile Blasting staff.

8. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Your information is never added to external mailing lists or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or other businesses.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our CRM or scheduling tools, under strict data protection requirements
  • If required by law or in response to a valid legal process

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors interact with our site. This information helps us improve our website experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at eran@showmemobileblasting.com or by phone at (573) 808-2771.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
